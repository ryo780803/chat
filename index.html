CONCEPT

ただいまと言いたくなる、
心の拠点。

Your Serene Home Away from Home

Hida Manu Houseは、飛騨高山の地で「暮らすように旅をする」を叶えるゲストハウス型民泊です。
高山観光、白川郷、飛騨古川、奥飛騨温泉郷など、人気観光スポットへのアクセスも良く、観光→温泉→宿でののんびりという最高の流れを楽しめる立地が魅力です。清潔感あふれる室内、使い勝手の良いキッチン、および大切な人と囲むダイニング。我が家のような安心感をご堪能ください。

At Hida Manu House, we offer a "live-like-a-local" experience in beautiful Hida Takayama. Perfectly positioned for exploring the historic streets of Takayama, Shirakawa-go, and the rejuvenating Okuhida hot springs, our house is the ideal base for your adventures. With pristine modern interiors, a fully-equipped kitchen, and a cozy dining area, you can enjoy a private, tranquil stay with your loved ones just as you would in your own home.

Conveniently located for visiting the "Old Town," Shirakawa-go, and Hida-Furukawa. After a day of sightseeing, unwind at a nearby hot spring before returning to the quiet comfort of Hida Manu House.
